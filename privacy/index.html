Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 December 2025

Overview

This site is operated by Lucian Irimie. I only collect limited, aggregated analytics to understand traffic and improve the site experience.

Data collected

  • Aggregated usage metrics (page views, device types, geography at region level) via Google Analytics, served through Cloudflare Zaraz.
  • Information you choose to submit through the contact form (name, email, message) so I can reply.

How analytics works

Google Analytics is loaded through Cloudflare Zaraz for performance and privacy. It is used only to understand site performance and engagement. Advertising and remarketing features are not enabled. Google Analytics may set cookies or anonymous identifiers for measurement, and Cloudflare Zaraz may be used for tag management.

Cookies and tracking

The site uses essential cookies and analytics cookies from Google Analytics (via Cloudflare Zaraz) to measure visits. No advertising cookies, fingerprinting, or profiling are used.

Sharing and retention

I do not sell your data. Analytics data is stored within Google Analytics and used in aggregate. Contact form submissions are retained only as needed to respond.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise your rights or ask a privacy question, email privacy@lucianirimie.com.
