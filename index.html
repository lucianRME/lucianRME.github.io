Synapse — GenAI for Requirements, BDD and Jira Readiness

Synapse demonstrates how Generative AI shortens refinement, improves clarity and reduces delivery friction — without changing governance, tooling or ways of working. It converts meeting transcripts into structured requirements, acceptance criteria and BDD scenarios, with optional Jira Cloud sync using session-only credentials.

Synapse on GPT Store — What it includes

Synapse – Meeting-to-Jira Assistant is the public demo in the ChatGPT Store. It guides you through a structured workflow to turn meeting notes/transcripts into draft user stories, acceptance criteria, and BDD scenarios you can copy into Jira.

  • Input: paste transcript snippets or bullet points (Teams/Meet/Zoom).
  • Processing: filters small talk, extracts entities, produces story templates + AC + BDD.
  • Outputs: copy-paste ready stories, simple tables, and quick summaries.
  • Scope: no API calls or data persistence in the public GPT (privacy-friendly demo).
