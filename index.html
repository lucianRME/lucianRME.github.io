Synapse — GenAI for Requirements, BDD and Jira Readiness

Synapse demonstrates how Generative AI shortens refinement, improves clarity and reduces delivery friction — without changing governance, tooling or ways of working. It converts meeting transcripts into structured requirements, acceptance criteria and BDD scenarios, with optional Jira Cloud sync using session-only credentials.

  • Faster story readiness from real meeting conversations
  • Clear, consistent acceptance criteria and BDD coverage
  • Improved backlog hygiene and predictability
  • Reduced ambiguity, hand-offs and rework
  • No installation and no enterprise data retained

Synapse on GPT Store — What it includes

The ChatGPT Store version guides you through a structured workflow to turn meeting notes and transcript snippets into draft user stories, acceptance criteria and BDD scenarios ready to copy into Jira.

  • Input: transcript or notes
  • Processing: filtering, structuring, story + AC + BDD
  • Output: ready-to-copy text
  • Privacy: no API calls, no storage
